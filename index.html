Pizza

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 1
Tomato Sauce 1
Cheese 1

Step 1: Roll out the dough to make a large circle shape.
Step 2: Put the tomato sauce on the pizza. Not too much and not too little.
Step 3: Grate the cheese and sprinkle it around the pizza.
Step 4: Put the pizza into the oven at 375 farenheit and cook for 30 minutes.

Source: How to make Pizza





Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread Slices 2
Ham Slice 1
Cheese Slice 1

Step 1: Put on slice of bread on the table.
Step 2: Then put one slice of ham on it.
Step 3: Then put one slice of cheese on it.
Step 4: Put the sandwich into the microwave and heat until desired.

S'mores

  • Time: 1 minute
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Grahm Crackers 2
Marshmallow 1
Chocolate 1 piece

Step 1: Lay one grahm cracker on the table.
Step 2: Lay the chocolate piece on top.
Step 3: Put the marshmallow on top of the chocolate.
Step 4: Put the second grahm cracker on top of the marshmallow.
Step 5: Put the s'more on the fire and let it cook until you want to eat it!

Source: How to make S'mores
